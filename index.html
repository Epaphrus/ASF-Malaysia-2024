ABOUT ASF2024

Welcome to Asia Smart Farming
& Food Security 2024

Asia Smart Farming & Food Security 2024 is your gateway to the future of agriculture — a meticulously curated event where cutting-edge technology meets sustainable farming practices. This year, we're proud to spotlight an extraordinary breakthrough: DNA No-Self Technology. This revolutionary approach, alongside our commitment to Integrated Pest Management (IPM) and Smart 4.0 Technologies, sets the stage for a deep dive into the nexus of innovation and sustainability. Our comprehensive program is designed to engage, enlighten, and empower. Participants will have the opportunity to explore the latest advancements in eco-friendly pest control, precision agriculture, and the groundbreaking DNA No-Self Technology, which promises to redefine our understanding of crop resilience and yield optimization. Through keynotes, workshops, and discussions, we'll examine how these innovations can be harmoniously integrated into sustainable agricultural practices.
